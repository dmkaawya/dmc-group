About DMC Group

Founded with a vision to create excellence across multiple industries, DMC Group has grown into a diversified conglomerate with operations in e-commerce, automotive, construction, real estate, and digital services.

With a team of 700 dedicated professionals, we are committed to delivering exceptional value to our customers through innovation, quality, and integrity in everything we do.

Our headquarters in Kalutara, Sri Lanka, serves as the strategic center for our operations across the country, enabling us to maintain the highest standards of service and delivery.
